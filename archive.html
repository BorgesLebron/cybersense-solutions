ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE // WORKFORCE
AI-assisted phishing reaches near-human quality
Large language models are now being used to craft spear-phishing emails that pass traditional detection filters. Security teams are responding by shifting from content-based filters to behavioral analysis — detecting the attempt, not the message. For most organizations, this means employee training on intent recognition becomes more valuable than technical filters alone.
SOURCE: CISA
QUANTUM COMPUTING // CRYPTOGRAPHY
NIST finalizes first post-quantum cryptography standards
NIST has published its first finalized post-quantum cryptographic standards, marking a critical transition point for organizations relying on current encryption methods. While quantum computers capable of breaking today's encryption are still years away, the migration window is narrow — organizations with long-lifecycle systems should begin inventory and planning now.
SOURCE: NIST
