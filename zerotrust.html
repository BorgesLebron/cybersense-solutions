Program Structure

Three Phases. One Measurable Outcome.

A sequenced program that builds knowledge, validates skill, and tests behavior under real adversarial conditions.

🎬
Phase 1

Video Training

Structured video curriculum delivered through the CyberSense platform, covering each of the 8 security domains. Each module includes a knowledge check quiz with a minimum 80% pass requirement before progression. Content is updated quarterly to reflect the current threat landscape – not the one from three years ago.

✓ Role-based learning tracks (technical vs. non-technical)
✓ Embedded knowledge checks per module
✓ Completion tracking on enterprise dashboard
✓ Mobile-accessible for distributed teams
🔬
Phase 2

Hands-On Labs

Eight security domain labs – each with realistic scenarios drawn from actual incident data, requiring employees to apply what they learned in Phase 1 in a simulated environment.

  • Email Security
  • Phishing & Social Engineering
  • Malware Awareness
  • Credential Management
  • Software Security
  • Cloud Security
  • Insider Threat
  • Endpoint Protection
⚡
Phase 3

Live Simulations

The final phase validates that training translated to real-world behavior under adversarial conditions. Our red team delivers targeted, realistic attack simulations against your employee population – coordinated with your security team but unknown to participants.

⚡ Email-based exploit simulations
⚡ Spear phishing with OSINT personalization
⚡ Vishing (voice call social engineering)
⚡ Smishing (SMS-based attack evaluation)
⚡ Post-simulation debrief & scoring
