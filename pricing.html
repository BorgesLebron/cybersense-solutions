Common questions

Is the free plan actually free?
Yes – no credit card required, no trial period. The Daily Digital Awareness Brief is free forever. Seven items every weekday morning by 07:00 CT.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access through the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions.
What's the difference between Standard and Premium?
Standard gives you full intelligence access – Radar, Intel Briefs, and the complete newsletter archive. Premium adds the Training Library, Professional Growth modules, and Innovation Library. If you're building technical skills alongside situational awareness, Premium is the right tier.
Is there a student or veteran discount?
Reach out at hello@cybersense.solutions. CyberSense is veteran-owned and we work with active duty, veterans, and students on a case-by-case basis.
When does the Enterprise platform launch?
October 2026. It includes team training modules, live phishing and social engineering simulations, a compliance dashboard, and SSO/SAML integration. Contact sales to get on the early access list.
