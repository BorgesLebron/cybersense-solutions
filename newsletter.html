When the Hospital Is the Target

UAC-0247 has been targeting Ukrainian government agencies and hospital systems – including emergency care facilities – with a campaign that begins with a humanitarian aid lure and ends with credential theft from Chromium-based browsers and WhatsApp. The campaign ran from March through April 2026. Clinics and emergency hospitals are not typical targets for financially motivated threat actors, which makes the targeting deliberate – these institutions hold patient data, operate on minimal security budgets, and cannot afford operational disruptions. Attacking them is a statement as much as it is an operation.
