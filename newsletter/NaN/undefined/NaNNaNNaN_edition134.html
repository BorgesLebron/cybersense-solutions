## Opening Brief

This edition examines a consistent and concerning pattern: foundational platforms that organizations depend on daily are increasingly becoming primary targets. From a claimed breach of a widely deployed learning management system to a structural change in enterprise patch management and a vulnerability in content authentication infrastructure, the common thread is institutional reliance. When trusted systems are compromised or disrupted, the downstream effects on operational continuity, data integrity, and user trust are significant. Defending against this pattern requires both disciplined technical hygiene and a workforce that understands the risks embedded in the tools they use.
